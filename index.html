74 Topics in Literary Theory

A Comprehensive Guide for Students and Scholars

*74 Topics in Literary Theory* offers a clear and structured introduction to the major schools of thought that have shaped literary studies. From foundational movements such as Structuralism and Formalism to later developments including Poststructuralism, Feminist Theory, Postcolonial Studies, Ecocriticism, and Digital Humanities, the book provides concise explanations of key concepts that continue to influence how we read and interpret literature today. Written with both clarity and academic depth, the book is designed for undergraduate and postgraduate courses in English literature, cultural studies, and comparative literature. It serves equally well as a classroom text, a revision companion, and a reference guide for students preparing for examinations or exploring theoretical frameworks for the first time.

Instructor resources—including lecture-ready PowerPoint slides and the full **PHI Learning Quiz App** for classroom engagement—are available to institutions that adopt the book for teaching.
